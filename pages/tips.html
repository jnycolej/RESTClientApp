Blackjack Tips

Here are some helpful tips for playing blackjack:

  • If your hand total is 8 or less, always hit.
  • If you have a hard hand (no Aces or Aces counted as 1), and your hand total is 12 to 16, hit if the dealer's upcard is 7 or higher. Otherwise, stand
  • If you have a hard hand, and your hand total is 17 or higher, always stand.
  • If you have a soft hand (includes an Ace counted as 11), follow basic strategy for hitting or standing based on the total.
  • If the dealer is showing a face card play on the assumption the reveal card is another face card or 10.
